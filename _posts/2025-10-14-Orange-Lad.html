I'm not sure when I adopted orange lad as my mascot, somewhere around 2016, but I do get asked a lot of questions about him.

FAQs

  1. What is that? He goes by a few names (Mr Orange, Orange Min) but I call him orange lad.
  2. Where is he from? He originated on a Zbrush central forum on 05.07.2005, where a user was just messing around to learn how to use the software. Then about 10 years later he took off as a minor internet meme. There is also a load of lore around lads and their subcultures, but it's all too gen Z for me.
  3. Why? I don't know
