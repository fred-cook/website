Surprisingly this is the first Bass pub I've reviewed, and on National Bass day no less. I have visited the Sandringham a few times before, it's my pub of choice if I have an hour or so to spare before a film at Showcase Avonmeads. This was a late afternoon Saturday Easter Bank Holiday visit, and the pub was lively
